Spring meeting Times: IURC will meet on Tuesdays at 6:45pm in the robotics lab on the 8th floor of Eigenmann (west wing).

The Indiana University Robotics Club is a group dedicated to the creation of working robots. We work to provide a forum for learning about everything from hardware to electronics to parts fabrication to programming. Members come from a variety of backgrounds and bring knowledge from their areas. If you are interested in getting started in robotics or are experienced in robotics and want to network with other enthusiasts, you should join us. Newcomers are always welcome.

For further information please send an e-mail to: atjacobs [AT] indiana.edu and someone should get back to you about when the next meeting is or about your specific question. You can also join our mailing list by sending an email to listserv@indiana.edu containing the following text in the body of the email:


subscribe roboclub_active YourFirstName YourLastName


But remember to put in your name in the appropriate places.
