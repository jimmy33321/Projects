RSS Feeds

News feeds from Indiana University are available using Really Simple Syndication (RSS). RSS is a syndicated news feed that provides access to article headlines, summaries, and links to full-text articles and resources on the web. Our news feeds can be read in your favorite news feed reader, also known as a news aggregator, or published to a personal or noncommercial website or blog.

To request a news feed to be created specifically for your department or unit, contact IU Communications News & Media at iuinfo@indiana.edu.

Please note the above feeds use RSS 2.0 ("rss20") which includes the title, link, published date, and description.

The "rss20" parameter can be replaced with "rss20IUCop" to replicate the Copenhagen news feeds which includes thumbnail images when available.

Another option is "rss20IUPUI" which will only display entries that have an image associated with them.
