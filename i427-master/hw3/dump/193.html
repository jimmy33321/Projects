Newsletters

It's hard to keep up with all the stories of how IU influences the state, nation, and world. Now you can customize your own news feed by subscribing to one or all of our e-newsletters, and following key social media accounts.

Stay in touch with the things you love about this great university. There is no cost, no advertising, and subscribers' email addresses will remain confidential with IU.

Five publications are currently available:

Everything IU
Everything IU is a bi-weekly e-newsletter, providing current, engaging information to people across the state and beyond who care about Indiana University. The e-newsletter encapsulates scholarly, research, cultural and entertainment activity from across the university system through feature stories, news highlights, photos, videos and event listings. It will also include some of the top news reports involving IU from around the state, nation and world, as well as the best blog postings from the IU News Room. Readers will find links to several key IU websites, such as the IU Alumni Association and Office of the President, the latest news about Hoosier sports teams and social media accounts to make sharing information with other friends of IU easy.

IU in the News
Stay on top of the latest, most important media coverage related to Indiana University. Each issue contain five to 10 of the biggest media headlines from the previous day, with afternoon updates being sent as news coverage warrants. In addition to links to the big news stories of the day, IU in the News also contains links to key IU websites and social media accounts if you want to catch the latest news and information being produced by the university. IU in the News will help cut through the clutter in your inbox by giving you a single source for the most important media headlines about the university during the week.

IU This Week
You can keep tabs on major headlines about faculty and staff on all of IU’s campuses with this newsletter, published on Fridays. It includes a summary of all the most important IU news. Headlines and links will keep you in touch with the latest developments in academics, athletics, and research.

Inside IU
Inside IU is Indiana University's bi-weekly, all-campus e-newsletter, providing current, relevant information to faculty and staff. This multimedia roundup, which launched Aug. 15, 2012, features stories and information on training and grant opportunities available, the quality and diversity of the IU workplace, IU faculty, student and staff, excellence and the positive effects the IU campuses have in their communities and throughout the world. Email us at iniu@iu.edu or give us a call at 812-855-6494.

Inside IU Bloomington
Inside IU Bloomington is the university’s bi-weekly e-newsletter, providing current, relevant information to faculty and staff on the Bloomington campus. This multimedia roundup, which launched January 2013, features stories and information on training and grant opportunities available, the quality and diversity of the IU workplace, IU faculty, student and staff, excellence and the positive effects the IU campuses have in their communities and throughout the world. Email us at iniub@indiana.edu or give us a call at 812-855-6494.
