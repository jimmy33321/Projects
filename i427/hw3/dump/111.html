Go on an intellectual journey that will change your life

An Indiana University Bloomington education transforms you.

Whether you’re an undergraduate or graduate student, whether you’re from South Bend, San Antonio, or Shanghai, you’ll encounter ideas and have experiences that help you refine your goals, develop the skills to reach them, and put you on the path to success.

Your transformation begins with our wide-ranging degrees.

An environment of exploration and achievement

At IU, we encourage you to break down barriers. Study and work with faculty across disciplines. Find a challenge so inspiring and daunting that you’re compelled to attack it from every angle. And take advantage of every experience that will help propel you to a successful career.

You’ll do all of it in a lively, international community of expert faculty and students who, like you, want to achieve great things—and want to help those around them do the same.

IU students who have shined

Entrepreneur and top M.B.A. student

Before Ramanuja Atur was one of two IU students named to Poets & Quants’ list of the world’s top 50 M.B.A. students in 2015, he co-founded a multinational software company in India. At the Kelley School of Business, he advised a nonprofit in Guatemala and interned for Amazon Web Services, which hired him full time.

Read about Ram

Student speaker caps IU career

Senior Mara González Souto spoke at 2015 Commencement. A political science, psychology, and French major, she founded an organization that raises awareness of unexploded land mines around the world. Mara will study international relations and politics at the University of Cambridge.

Read about Mara

Big honor for big-screen director

Variety named Media School doctoral student Russell Sheaffer one of “110 Students to Watch” in 2015. He has produced, directed, edited, or co-written dozens of films, many of which have screened at festivals such as Sundance and Tribeca. He’s also helped organize festivals at the IU Cinema.
