Admissions

As an international student, you demand (and deserve) a lot from a university. You need an institution with a first-class reputation, highly ranked academic programs, and global connections.

You will find everything you are looking for and more at Indiana University.

Download the OIS Viewbook »

Learning at Indiana University

Business. Music. Law. Education. These are just a few of the top-ranked programs at IU. You can also choose from more than 70 language programs, 150 majors, and hundreds of internship opportunities.

Learn more about academics »

Living at Indiana University

Connect with students from all over the United States and across the globe. Immerse yourself in the arts and new cultures. See what living in a classic American college town is really like—but remain close to large cities like Chicago, Illinois, and Louisville, Kentucky. There is no end to the opportunities and experiences that await you.

Learn more about life here »

Applying to Indiana University

Take the first step toward becoming an IU student by applying online. Complete your application as early as you can—the priority deadline for some Selective Scholarships is November 1. Otherwise, first year (freshmen) international students who complete their application by February 1 may also be eligible for competitive, merit-based scholarships unique to internationals.
