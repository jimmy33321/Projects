IU Bloomington is committed to addressing sexual violence on campus, and today is releasing results from its first survey on the issue.

The information from this survey is already being used to to inform and improve the university’s ongoing prevention, education and response effort related to all forms of sexual misconduct, including hosting a statewide conference about sexual assault prevention with student leaders this month at IUPUI.

We welcome your input on the climate survey or more generally on this issue at care@indiana.edu. To learn more about IU Bloomington’s ongoing work to address sexual violence, we encourage you to visit stopsexualviolence.iu.edu.﻿

Introduction
Founded in 1820, Indiana University is the state's flagship university and Bloomington is its flagship campus. Located on a beautiful residential campus in a delightful small Midwestern city, IU offers the quintessential college experience.
