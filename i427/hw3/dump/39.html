Visiting Bloomington

Bring your family to meet ours

When you’re a Hoosier, your family is our family. We hope that you give them a chance to visit you and get to know us.

They’re welcome anytime, of course. But each fall, we devote two weekends to the celebration of parents and family.

Prospective student visits

Looking to schedule a tour of our gorgeous campus? We’ve got all the information you need.

Freshman Family Weekend

In September, we give the parents and families of freshman students the cream and crimson treatment during Freshman Family Weekend.

It gives our newest Hoosiers a chance to show off their campus, and to share what they’ve already learned about life in Bloomington.

Events include special tours, an IU football game tailgate party, and a special brunch.

This year’s Freshman Family Weekend is September 18–20.

Learn more about Freshman Family Weekend

Parents Weekend

In early November, we invite the parents of students at every level—freshman, senior, and even graduate—to join us for a weekend devoted to them.

This year’s Parents Weekend is November 5–7.

Get more details about Parents Weekend

It’s always a great time to visit Bloomington

Bloomington is gorgeous no matter what the season. We’re famous for eye-popping displays of fall color and spring blooms. But you’ll find just as much to love while crunching across our snow-covered campus in winter or taking a leisurely stroll through downtown in the summer.
