550+ academic programs, including the right one (or more) for you

Our academic programs are broad, deep, and interdisciplinary. Many are among the best of their kind. They’re also flexible so you can combine them into your ideal program of study. Undergraduates can even create an individualized major.

So what will you study at IU?

In addition to our 550+ stand-alone degrees and certificates, we offer options like area certificates that must be earned in conjunction with a degree.
