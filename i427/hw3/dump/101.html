Attend HLC's Professional Development Week

Registration is now open for HLC's Professional Development Week, being held February 9–12, 2016, in Scottsdale, Arizona. The event offers faculty and administrators key information and strategies designed to spur institutional improvement in the areas of assessment of student learning and student persistence and completion. Representatives from HLC-accredited and candidate institutions are invited to attend as institutional teams or individual participants. Learn more and register today >>
